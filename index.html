About the coffee shop

Our Coffee Shop is located in the Practicum Library's hall. It features 6 tables and free WIFI.

Our main mission is keeping guests cozy and engaged. Beside that, we try to reduce our environmental impact-so we don't sell single-use cup. But you can buy one of our reusable cups.
